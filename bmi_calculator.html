Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator

The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measurement that helps assess whether an individual is underweight, at a healthy weight, overweight, or obese based on their height and weight.

Calculate Your BMI

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 - 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 - 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or greater

Remember, while BMI is a useful indicator, it does not account for muscle mass, bone density, and overall body composition. It's always a good idea to consult with a healthcare professional for a more accurate assessment of your health.
